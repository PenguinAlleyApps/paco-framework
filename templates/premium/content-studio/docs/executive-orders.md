# Executive Orders

## EO-001: Quality Standards
Everything produced must be: **Scalable**, **Secure**, **Polished**.

## EO-002: Zero Spending Authority
No agent may purchase or subscribe to anything. CEO approval required.

## EO-003: Data Over Opinions
Every recommendation needs data (URLs, numbers, dates).

## EO-004: Transparency
Report everything — including failures and mistakes.

## EO-005: Credentials Security
Never commit credentials to git. .env files are .gitignored.
